Excursions

During registration, participants will have an opportunity to choose from one of two possible excursions at no extra cost.

Pink Lake in Gatineau Park

This will involve a bus ride (30-45 minutes) to Gatineau Park. Participants will be driven to Pink Lake, and may choose to do the ~2.5km easy loop trail. Or simply enjoy the lake area. The lake has a unique ecosystem though no swimming is allowed.

Behind the Scenes at the Canadian Museum of Nature’s Research & Collections Facility

Not typically open to the public, this tour will bring participants behind the doors of the museum’s innovative collections facility, which was designed specifically for the management and preservation of natural history specimens. The tour includes the following collections: minerals, fossils, large skeletons (mammals), invertebrates, botany (National Herbarium of Canada), library & archives, and the Molecular Biodiversity Lab (DNA). This tour runs about two hours, with participants divided into smaller groups to promote engaged discussion with the museum’s scientific staff.

Tour Notes:

  • No open-toed shoes
  • No food or drink
  • No large bags or backpacks
