Logistics

Airport

The Ottawa International Aiport (YOW) is 12km south of the downtown core and is Canada's sixth busiest airport. Because of Canada 150 celebrations throughout 2017, it will experience heavier volumes than usual. Plan accordingly. It serves many major North American airlines and has flights to Europe and several cities in the United States. However, most flights require a connecting flight through Toronto (YYZ) or Montréal (YUL).

Train

The Ottawa Station (XDS), located at 200 Trembley Road, is served by Via Rail to Toronto and Montréal. Air France–KLM runs a connecting shuttle bus from this station to the Montréal Airport (YUL) as an alternate mode of transportation to/from Ottawa.

Transit

OC Transpo bus route 97 provides frequent express service to downtown Ottawa along a dedicated transitway. After 50 minutes of travel, it makes a stop on Albert Street between Kent Street and Lyon Street North, a half block away from the Delta Ottawa City Centre hotel main entrance. Fare is $3.40CAD cash (coins) or two $1.70CAD tickets. Bus tickets are available at the ground transportation desk in the airport, located on level one at the central door of the arrivals area. Although this is a commuter bus, drivers are accustomed to accommodating travellers with luggage. However, if you plan on taking the bus to the airport after the conference during rush hour, we recommend alternate forms of transportation.

Taxi & Uber

A 25-30min, metered taxi ride to the Delta Ottawa City Centre hotel from the airport will cost approximately $35CAD. Major credit cards are accepted onboard. Uber is available in Ottawa; a ride to the Delta Ottawa City Centre hotel from the airport will cost $19-25CAD.

Climate

The city of Ottawa sits within the St. Lawrence Lowlands ecoregion, characterized by warm summers and cold snowy winters, mixedwood forests of sugar maple, yellow birch, eastern hemlock, and eastern white pine. Average daily high/low temperatures in October are 15°C/3°C with a monthly average of 90mm of rain over 16 days.
